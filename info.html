Robbe is a 3D and motion design artist with over eight years of experience creating concept-driven work for beauty, fashion, and tech clients. His practice is rooted in 3D and animation, built on a foundation in illustration, graphic design, and 2D motion.


After several years at UK studio ILOVEDUST — contributing to campaigns for Apple, Nike, Spotify, and Google — he went freelance in 2022 and now works from Prague with studios like ZUNC, Sucuk & Bratwurst, and Softpower.
